Schematics are [NBT](/nbt) files that can store custom blueprints for [Minecraft](/minecraft) builds.

Schematics [Mods](/mod) usually provide a way better editing user experience than the [Vanilla](/vanilla) way to edit [NBT](/nbt) files with the [Structure blocks](/structureblock).

The most popular schematic [Mods](/mod) are [Litematica](https://modpackker.vercel.app/project/litematica) and [Create](https://modpackker.vercel.app/project/create).
