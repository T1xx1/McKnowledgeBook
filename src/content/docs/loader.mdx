Loaders are custom software designed to replace some parts of the [Vanilla](/vanilla) engine to provide custom functionality and extensibility to [Minecraft](/minecraft).

There are different types of loaders:

- [Mod loaders](/modloader)
- [Plugin loaders](/pluginloader)
- [Shader loaders](/shaderloader)
- [Server engine](/serverengine)
- [Proxy servers](/proxyserver)
