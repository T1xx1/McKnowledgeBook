Shaders are a [Project type](./projecttype) that improve the visual quality of the game by adding advanced lighting, shadows, reflections, and other effects.

They require a [Loader](/modloader) called [Shader loader](/shaderloader) to work.

:::tip[News]
[Vibrant visuals](/vibrantvisuals) may change the requirements for shaders to work. [Read more](./vibrantvisuals#modding)
:::
